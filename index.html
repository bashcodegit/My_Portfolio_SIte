About Bashid.

Hello, I'm Bashid, hailing from the picturesque land of Malappuram, Kerala. Armed with a B.Tech in Computer Science and a passion for technology, I'm on a journey to make an impact in the digital realm. With a solid foundation in Computer Science, further solidified by my completion of RedHat courses, I have honed my skills as a server engineer and cloud management enthusiast.

My expertise extends into the realm of web design and web development, where I thrive in creating visually captivating and user-centric experiences. Armed with a relentless drive, I'm steadfastly working towards becoming a DevOps engineer, combining my technical prowess with a deep understanding of development and operations. From code to cloud, I'm dedicated to pushing the boundaries of technology to deliver seamless, innovative solutions.
